## 🛒 Blinkit Outlet Performance Analysis

### 📌 Problem Statement

The objective was to analyze outlet performance across different tiers, outlet types, and product categories to identify opportunities for improving sales and overall business performance.

---

### 🛠️ Tools Used

* Power BI
* Data Modeling (Star Schema)
* DAX

---

### 📊 Dataset

* ₹1.2M+ total sales
* 8K+ items
* Data included: outlet type, tier, product category, sales, and item count

---

### 🔍 Key Analysis Performed

* Compared **sales performance across Tier 1, Tier 2, and Tier 3 outlets**
* Evaluated **outlet type performance (Supermarket vs Grocery Store)**
* Analyzed **category-wise contribution to total sales**
* Studied **average order behavior across outlet types**

---

### 💡 Key Insights

Analysis revealed key differences in outlet and category performance:

* **Tier 3 outlets contributed highest total sales (~3000)** compared to Tier 1 (~2000)
* **Tier 1 outlets showed higher Average Order Value (AOV)**, indicating fewer but higher-value purchases
* **Supermarkets outperformed grocery stores** in both revenue and transaction volume
* **Fruits and Snacks were top-performing categories**, driving most of the revenue

---

### 🚀 Business Recommendations (with Reason)

* Increase **product variety in Tier 1 outlets**
  → Reason: Higher AOV indicates customers are willing to spend more, so more options can increase total sales

* Focus on **promotions and visibility for grocery stores**
  → Reason: Lower performance compared to supermarkets suggests need for demand generation

* Strengthen **high-performing categories like snacks with combo offers**
  → Reason: Leveraging already popular categories can further boost revenue

---

### 📊 Success Measurement (Expected Impact)

* Potential increase in **sales volume in Tier 1 outlets**
* Improvement in **grocery store performance and revenue share**
* Higher **sales contribution from key categories through targeted promotions**

---

### 📌 Business Impact (Potential)

* Identified opportunities to improve **sales distribution across outlet tiers**
* Insights can support **better product assortment and promotional strategies**
* Recommendations may help increase **overall revenue and category performance**
